Good coffee shouldn't cost the Earth

We are the CoolBeans Coffee Collective - a movement which has its roots in Belfast, Northern Ireland, but is now spreading across the UK and Ireland. Our sole aim is to bring the best flavours to our customers, while upholding ethical values.

We work direcly with farmers, pickers and distributers across the globe to ensure that everyone is paid a fair price for their work, and we only source from farms who seek to share our vision of minimal envinronmental impact. We are the only coffee company in Europe to use fully-biodegradable coffee cups, made from 100% locally-sourced recycled cardboard.
